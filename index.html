Who we are

Electrified car club aims to be the world’s best EV Car Club. Our club is limited to EV owners, as we wanted a place where people who owned EV’s could talk to, meet, and hangout with other EV owners. What differentiates us from other car clubs is we aren’t about who has the most mods, who has spent the most money on their car, or really even if you want to modify your car (though we do encourage that). We’re a community of people who enjoy each others companies, and want to give back to our communities where we can. We strive to be a positive influence in the communities we are in, which is why we have the rules and bylaws that we do, to ensure that negative influences don’t remain in the club and don’t deter from what we’re here to do. While we host many events, and attend many others, the Community Events are what we take the most pride in, we want to be the positive change makers in our community and want to be able to give back and support those around us and those give us the ability to do just that.

Where we started

Electrified Car Club started in 2021 originally as Electrified Orlando. We were having trouble finding spots, car meets, and others who would embrace us EV owners as fellow car owners as well. So we aimed to build a club where we could have a group of us to showcase that it wasn’t just one or two people, but this was in fact part of the future. As we started doing more events, and posting more around social media people have reached out asking us if they could start a chapter, or what we are about because they were interested in what we’re doing. So upon this happening, we rebranded the club a Electrified Car Club, and have taken Electrified Orlando and made it our founding chapter, and have been happy to welcome new chapters on since doing this.

What we do

Community Events

These are events where we invite anyone in the club, and people with EV’s who are interested, and do various things, something like Batteries and Brunch, EV Meet and Rally, and our EV Meet with Tint World Orlando. These events bring the community together and allow us to hangout and enjoy ourselves, talk, and meet other owners.

Club Events

Community events are events where we give back to the community, we’ve held various events like a Rally and Food Drive for the local food bank, Orlando has done 3 Frunk or Treat’s at the Ronald McDonald House of Central Florida, providing a place for families and kids to be able to have fun and enjoy themselves.

Car Meets

These are your typical car meets, where we typically go to meets hosted by other groups and try and bring a group of cars (usually modified, or unique) to hangout and showcase what the current EV’s are, and what real owners are doing to them.
